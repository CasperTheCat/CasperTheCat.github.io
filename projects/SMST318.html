SMST318 Project

Style

For my project, I propose a silhouette style animation reminiscent of shadow puppetry. I want to explore this style as it has an inheritant minimalist simplicity that I really feel gives an ambience.

It is worth noting that I will be doing this in 3D as I feel that I can work better in a 3D setting and I feel it will allow for a more fluid end result.

First Test

This was my proof of concept video, total scene setup and rendering time came in at just under 3 hours. Most of this was due to me trying to work out the best workflow for the rest of the project. I also needed to learn about Maya 2017's render layer overrides to get the silhouette material applied to all objects globally.

Second Test

This is a continuation on the exploration done previously, this took under 2 hours including planning to get this rendered into 5 passes and composited. In hindsight, I could actually have used one pass and it would have saved a lot of time on the compositng side.
